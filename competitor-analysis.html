The "empty your head & crumple it" note app

A lock‑screen‑first, ultra‑minimal post‑it app. You dump a fleeting to‑do in ≤40 characters, glance at it on your lock screen, and crumple it away (confetti ✨) when it's done — no checkboxes, no projects, no account.

Positioning in one line: Everyone else helps you manage tasks; 구깃 helps you forget them. It sits at the far low‑friction / disposable end of the spectrum — opposite to power task managers like Things 3 / Todoist, and lighter & more playful than Apple Notes / Google Keep.

✅ Where 구깃 wins

  • Fastest possible capture — no fields, no setup, ≤40 chars.
  • Lock‑screen native — the note lives where you actually glance.
  • Disposable by design — "crumple" beats an ever‑growing archive.
  • Emotional / fun — confetti + paper feel; not a chore app.
  • Private & free — local‑only, no account, no upsell.
  • Color = category — lightweight grouping without folders.

⚠️ Trade‑offs / risks

  • No reminders/dates — not for time‑sensitive tasks.
  • No sync — single device, no backup if phone is lost.
  • 40‑char + no edit — wrong fit for longer or evolving notes.
  • iOS‑only, niche — small addressable market vs giants.
  • Easy to copy — the idea is simple; defensibility = taste & feel.
  • Discovery — "do I need another note app?" is the hurdle.
